Thunderous Lasagna

A delicious lasagna made by Thor himself.

The carefully gratinated cheese, thanks to Thor's Mjolnir will electrify your senses.

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Greek Tomatoes
  • Italian Pasta

Preparation steps:

  1. Take Thor's Mjolnir and melt the cheese
  2. Liquefy the tomatoes with Odin's help
  3. Insert the previous mix among the layers of Italian Pasta
  4. Enjoy!
