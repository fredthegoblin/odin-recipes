Deceitful Horse Meat

Succulent, but shady, horse meat made by Loki himself.

The patiently cooked horse meat, thanks to Odin's ruses, will melt inside your mouth.

Ingredients:

  • Horse Meat
  • Basil

Preparation steps:

  1. Convince Loki to steal and cook a horse for you
  2. Add the basil
  3. Enjoy!
